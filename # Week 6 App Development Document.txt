Describe the world and context. This may be real, fantasy, science fiction, personal, professional, or practical.
	- A world in the far future is struck by two cataclysmic events the first sent humanity back hundreds of years, the second melded a magical and supernatural realm to our own. Many years after this event humans have found a way to coexist with their new melded world. A large group of explorers called the Routers Guild are responsible for going out into the new world and gathering information on what is out there. They use very primitive modules (early laptop computers) to manually connect to other hubs and to take down information and track objectives.

What happened before the app exists?
